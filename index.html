Think about yourself

By Maíra Araújo

What series do you like watching?
Why do you watch them?
When do you usually watch them?

Have you ever thought about the people behind it?
The writers, actors, directors...
How would your life be nowadays without them?

In my case, I watch it to scape from reality. To find some distraction. To feel emotions that I would never feel in real life. It is almost like if I could live what is happening in some episodes and completely forget about my problems and duties (maybe this part is not a very good reason).

So here I am to tell you about Shonda Rhimes. My personal Hero.
And no, she cannot read minds, she is not super strong, she cannot fly. But she has a great imagination.
She has to study a lot about heavy subjects in order to create the best series I've known. She must have a deep knowledge about medicine and american law in these two particular cases that I will be talking about, and sometimes I even think she knows more than a last year student. This woman is a GENIUS.
